  1. BOTIVATE KYA HAI?
================================================================================

Botivate ek smart meeting management system hai. Isko aap apna personal
secretary samajh sakte hain, jo:

  - Aapki saari meetings ka record rakhta hai.
  - Aapki meeting ki recording sunkar automatically likh deta hai ki
    meeting mein kya kya discuss hua.
  - Discussion mein se action items (tasks) khud identify karke sahi
    logon ko assign kar deta hai.
  - Sabhi attendees ko ek professional email bhejta hai jisme complete
    meeting summary aur PDF document attached hota hai.
  - Jinko task mila hai, unko alag email jaata hai jisme unka task
    aur uski deadline likhi hoti hai.
  - Track karta hai ki tasks time pe ho rahe hain ya deadline nikal
    gayi hai.
  - Meeting ke saare documents (recordings, transcripts, summaries)
    Google Drive mein safely store karta hai.
  - Charts aur statistics dikhata hai jisse aapko meeting trends aur
    task completion rates samajhne mein asaani ho.

System do tarah ki meetings handle karta hai:
  a) Regular Meetings (jaise team meetings, project discussions)
  b) Board Resolutions (BR) (jaise company ke formal decisions,
     compliance ke kaam)

================================================================================
  3. HOME SCREEN (DASHBOARD) KO SAMJHEIN
================================================================================

Jab aap Botivate kholte hain, sabse pehla screen Dashboard dikhta hai.
Ye aapka command center hai. Har section kya dikhata hai:

--- UPAR BAAYIN TARAF (Neela Gradient Box) ---
  "Next Scheduled Meeting"
  Ye bada, highlighted box aapko dikhata hai ki aapki agali meeting kab hai.
  Aap dekh sakte hain:
    - Meeting ka naam/title
    - Date aur time
    - Jagah ya online link
    - Kitne log invited hain
  Yahaan do buttons hain:
    [View Details] - Meeting ki poori jaankari dekhne ke liye click karein.
    [Record MOM]   - Is meeting ka MOM likhna shuru karne ke liye click karein.

--- UPAR DAAYIN TARAF (Safed Box) ---
  "Last Meeting"
  Ye chhota box dikhata hai ki sabse recently kaunsi meeting hui thi.
  Aap dekh sakte hain:
    - Meeting ka naam
    - Kis date ko hui thi
    - Kitne tasks complete hue vs total tasks
    - Kitne log aaye the vs total invited
  Neeche "View all meetings" pe click karke poori meetings list dekh
  sakte hain.

--- DOOSRI ROW (Purple/Indigo Gradient Box) ---
  "Next Board Resolution"
  Upar wale meeting box jaise hi hai, par ye khaas taur pe aapki
  agle Board Resolution ke liye hai. Isme bhi [View Details] aur
  [Record MOM] buttons hain.

--- DOOSRI ROW DAAYIN TARAF (Safed Box) ---
  "Last Board Resolution"
  Sabse recently complete hui Board Resolution ki details dikhata hai,
  jisme kitne action items complete hue aur current status kya hai.

--- STATISTICS WALI ROW (7 Chhote Rang-birangi Boxes) ---
  Ye chhote cards important numbers dikhate hain ek nazar mein:
    - Total Meetings:  System mein kitni meetings hain total.
    - Total Tasks:     Saari meetings mein milaakar kitne tasks hain.
    - Pending:         Jo tasks abhi shuru nahi hue.
    - In Progress:     Jo tasks pe koi kaam kar raha hai abhi.
    - Completed:       Jo tasks ho chuke hain.
    - Overdue:         Jo tasks ki deadline nikal gayi aur abhi tak nahi hue.
    - Upcoming:        Kitni meetings future mein schedule hain.

--- CHARTS SECTION ---
  Do visual charts ek saath dikhte hain:

  Baayin Chart - "Meeting Trends":
    Ek bar chart jo dikhata hai ki har mahine mein kitni meetings hui.
    Jitni lambi bar, utni zyada meetings us mahine mein.

  Daayin Chart - "Task Distribution":
    Ek golaakaar (donut) chart jo dikhata hai ki Pending, In Progress,
    aur Completed tasks ka anupaat kya hai. Rangon se ek nazar mein
    samajh aa jaata hai ki zyada tasks ho chuke hain ya abhi pending hain.

--- NEECHE KA SECTION ---
  Do lists ek saath dikhti hain:

  Baayin List - "Recent Meetings":
    Pichhli kuch meetings dikhata hai unke naam, date, jagah, aur
    kitne tasks the. Kisi bhi meeting ke naam pe click karke uski
    poori details dekh sakte hain.
    Poori list ke liye "View all" pe click karein.

  Daayin List - "Overdue Tasks":
    Woh tasks dikhata hai jinki deadline nikal chuki hai. Har entry
    mein dikhta hai:
      - Task ka naam
      - Kaun zimmedaar hai
      - Kab tak karna tha
    Agar koi overdue task nahi hai, toh khushi ka message "No overdue
    tasks" aata hai ek celebration icon ke saath.


================================================================================
  4. NAYI MEETING KAISE SCHEDULE KAREIN
================================================================================

Ye meeting lifecycle ka pehla step hai. Schedule karne ka matlab hai
meeting ki date, time, aur kaun aayega ye set karna. Step-by-step process:

Step 1: Kisi bhi page pe, baayin sidebar mein "Meetings" pe click karein.

Step 2: Meetings page ke upar, [Schedule Meeting] button pe click karein.
        Aap "Schedule New Meeting" form pe pahunch jayenge.

Step 3: "Meeting Details" section bharein:

  Title (Zaroori hai):
    Meeting ka naam likhein. Jaise: "Weekly Team Sync" ya
    "Budget Review Q2". Ye field zaroori hai - iske bina submit
    nahi hoga.

  Organization:
    Aapki company ya organization ka naam. Ye pehle se
    "Botivate Services LLP" bhara hota hai, par aap badal sakte hain.

  Meeting Type:
    Dropdown se select karein ki ye kaisi meeting hai:
      - Regular Meeting (default)
      - Board Resolution (agar ye select kiya, toh meeting Board
        Resolutions section mein banegi, regular Meetings mein nahi)
      - Committee Meeting
      - Annual General Meeting

  Meeting Mode:
    Chunein ki meeting kaise hogi:
      - Online (video call ke liye, jaise Google Meet, Zoom wagairah)
      - Offline (aamne-saamne meeting ke liye)

  Venue / Platform:
    Agar Meeting Mode "Online" hai, toh video call ka link daalein
    (jaise: "https://meet.google.com/abc-defg-hij").
    Agar Meeting Mode "Offline" hai, toh physical jagah likhein
    (jaise: "Conference Room A, 3rd Floor").

  Date:
    Date field pe click karein aur calendar se meeting ki date
    chunein.

  Time:
    Time field pe click karein aur meeting ka time set karein.

  Hosted By:
    Meeting ki chair karne wale ya host karne wale ka naam likhein.
    Jaise: "Satyendra Sir (CEO)".

Step 4: "Agenda" section bharein:
  Discussion ke topics add karne ke liye [+ Add] button pe click karein.
  Har topic ke liye likhein:
    - Topic: Ek chhota title (jaise "Project Update")
    - Description: Thoda detail mein ki kya discuss hoga
  Aur topics add karne ke liye dubara [+ Add] pe click karein.
  Koi topic hatane ke liye uske aage laal [Remove] button pe click karein.

Step 5: "Expected Attendees" section bharein:
  Har aadmi ko add karne ke liye [+ Add] button pe click karein.
  Har attendee ke liye bharein:

    Unique ID:
      Is aadmi ki pehchaan ka number (jaise employee ID).

    Name (Zaroori hai):
      Attendee ka poora naam. Jaise: "Prabhat Kumar Singh".

    Email:
      Unka email address jahan pe meeting ka invitation bheja jayega.
      Jaise: "prabhat@company.com".
      ZAROORI BAAT: Agar aap email daalte hain, toh system automatically
      ek professional meeting invitation email bhejega is aadmi ko!

    Designation:
      Unki job title. Jaise: "AI Developer" ya "Director".

    WhatsApp Number:
      Unka WhatsApp number contact ke liye.

    Remarks:
      Is khaas attendee ke liye koi personal note ya instruction.
      Jaise: "Q2 report lekar aana" ya "Presentation tayyar rakhna".
      DHYAN DEIN: Ye remarks invitation email mein SHAMIL honge jo
      is attendee ko jayega, toh woh aapka note dekh sakenge.

  Kisi attendee ko hatane ke liye laal [Remove Attendee] button pe
  click karein.

Step 6: Neeche bade neele button pe click karein:
        [Schedule & Send Invites]

Iske baad kya hota hai:
  - Meeting system mein save ho jaati hai.
  - Har attendee jiska email address hai, usko ek professionally
    designed meeting invitation email jaata hai jisme:
      - Meeting ka title
      - Date aur time
      - Jagah/Platform
      - Unka personal remark (agar aapne likha ho)
  - Aap automatically Meeting Details page pe pahunch jayenge.
  - Ek safalta ka message "Meeting scheduled successfully! Invitations
    sent." dikhega.

  5. MEETINGS LIST PAGE KO SAMJHEIN
================================================================================

Jab aap baayin sidebar mein "Meetings" pe click karte hain, toh aapki
saari regular meetings ki list dikhti hai (Board Resolutions nahi -
woh alag hain).

--- UPAR KE BUTTONS ---
  Teen buttons dikhte hain upar:

  [Upload MOM]:
    Isse use karein agar meeting ka audio/video recording system ke
    baahar record hua ho aur aap use yahan upload karna chahte hain.

  [Schedule Meeting]:
    Scheduling form pe le jaata hai (Section 4 mein samjhaya gaya hai).

  [Create MOM]:
    Isse use karein agar aap seedha meeting ki details aur MOM ek
    saath manually likhna chahte hain, bina recording ke.

--- TASK INTELLIGENCE SUMMARY ---
  Upar ek bar dikhta hai jo saari meetings ke tasks ki statistics dikhata hai:
    - Total Tasks: Saari meetings mein milaakar kitne tasks hain
    - Pending: Kitne tasks shuru hone ka intezaar kar rahe hain
    - In Progress: Kitne tasks pe kaam ho raha hai
    - Completed: Kitne tasks ho chuke hain
  In numbers pe click karke aap meetings ko filter kar sakte hain
  jo us status ke tasks wali hain.

--- TAB FILTERS ---
  Teen tabs se aap meetings ko unki haalat ke hisaab se filter kar
  sakte hain:

  [Upcoming]:
    Woh meetings dikhata hai jo schedule ho chuki hain par abhi hui
    nahi hain, ya system abhi unhe process kar raha hai.

  [Completed]:
    Woh meetings dikhata hai jinka MOM final ho chuka hai aur save
    ho gaya hai.

  [Cancelled]:
    Woh meetings dikhata hai jo cancel kar di gayi theen.

--- MEETING CARDS ---
  Har meeting ek card ki tarah dikhti hai jisme:
    - Meeting Title (bada text upar)
    - Organization ka naam
    - Date aur Time
    - Jagah / Platform
    - Status badge (Scheduled, Processing, Completed, Cancelled, wagairah)
      "Processing" ka matlab hai system abhi meeting ki recording ko
      analyze kar raha hai.
    - Kitne attendees hain
    - Task counts (Pending, In Progress, Completed)

  Har card pe Action Buttons:
    [View] - Poori meeting details dekhne ke liye click karein.
    [Record MOM] - Is meeting ka MOM likhne ke liye click karein.
    [Delete] (laal kachre ka icon) - Meeting ko hamesha ke liye
      delete karne ke liye click karein. Delete karne se pehle ek
      confirmation box aayega.


================================================================================
  6. MOM KAISE RECORD / LOG KAREIN (MINUTES OF MEETING)
================================================================================

Meeting ho jaane ke baad, aapko minutes "log" ya "record" karne hote hain.
Yahaan aap capture karte hain ki meeting mein kya hua.

Step 1: Meetings page pe jaayein (sidebar mein "Meetings" pe click karein).

Step 2: Woh meeting dhundhein jiska MOM likhna hai.

Step 3: Meeting card pe [Record MOM] pe click karein.
        (Aap pehle [View] pe bhi click kar sakte hain, phir details
        page se [Record MOM] pe click karein.)

Step 4: Aapko "Meeting Details" page dikhega jisme ye sections honge:

  --- AUDIO RECORDING ---
  Upar ek recording widget dikh sakta hai. Isse aap:
    - Microphone button pe click karke apne computer ke microphone se
      seedha audio record kar sakte hain.
    - Meeting ki discussion khatam hone pe recording rok dein.
    - System automatically is recording ko upload aur process karega.
    YA
    - Aap pehle se recorded audio file (.webm, .mp3, .m4a) ko upload
      area mein drag aur drop kar sakte hain.

  --- MARK ATTENDANCE ---
  Aapko saare invited attendees ki list dikhegi. Har aadmi ke liye:
    - Aap unka naam aur email dekh sakte hain.
    - Unke naam ke saath dropdown se mark karein:
        "Present" - Woh meeting mein aaye the.
        "Absent"  - Woh nahi aaye the.
        "Excused" - Unki nahi aane ki koi jaayaz wajah thi.
    - Aap optional "Personal Remark" bhi likh sakte hain har attendee ke liye.

  --- DISCUSSION SUMMARY ---
  Ek bada text box jahan aap likh sakte hain ki meeting mein kya
  discuss hua.
  Agar aapne audio upload ya record kiya hai, toh ye box AI ke
  process karne ke baad automatically bhar jayega.
  Aap hamesha summary ko manually edit kar sakte hain.

  --- ACTION ITEMS / TASKS ---
  [+ Add Task] pe click karke meeting mein decide hue tasks add karein.
  Har task ke liye bharein:
    - Task Title: Kya karna hai (jaise "Q2 Report Tayyar Karo")
    - Assigned To: Kaun karega. Aap ya toh:
        - Attendees ki dropdown list se chun sakte hain, YA
        - Manually naam type kar sakte hain agar woh aadmi meeting
          mein nahi tha
    - Email: Zimmedaar aadmi ka email (dropdown se chunne pe
      automatically bhar jaata hai)
    - Deadline: Kis date tak ye task complete hona chahiye, date
      chunne ke liye click karein
    - Status: Abhi ki haalat chunein:
        "Pending"     - Abhi shuru nahi hua
        "In Progress" - Kaam chal raha hai
        "Completed"   - Ho chuka hai
  Task hatane ke liye laal [Remove] button pe click karein.

  --- NEXT MEETING ---
  Optional: Agle follow-up meeting ki date aur time set kar sakte hain.

Step 5: Neeche ke bade hare button pe click karein:
        [Save & Finalize MOM]

Iske baad kya hota hai:
  - Saara data save ho jaata hai.
  - Agar audio upload/record kiya tha, toh AI system ise process
    karta hai:
      1. Audio ko text mein badla jaata hai (transcription).
      2. Text se professional meeting notes ki summary banayi jaati hai.
      3. Teen PDF documents bante hain:
           a) Poora shabdakshara transcript (har bol gaya shabd)
           b) AI processing audit logs
           c) Executive summary briefing
      4. Saare documents Google Drive mein upload hote hain.
      5. Sabhi attendees ko MOM summary aur PDF attachment ke saath
         email jaata hai.
      6. Jinko task mila hai, unko alag email jaata hai jisme unka
         task aur uski deadline likhi hoti hai.
  - Meeting ka status "Completed" ho jaata hai.

  7. PEHLE SE RECORDED FILE KAISE UPLOAD KAREIN

  - File system mein upload ho jaati hai.
  - AI pipeline processing shuru kar deta hai (Section 6 mein
    bataya gaya waisa hi).
  - Meeting ka status "Processing" ho jaata hai jab tak AI kaam
    kar raha hota hai.
  - Hone ke baad status "Completed" ho jaata hai ya "Processing"
    mein rehta hai taaki aap Record MOM page se attendance aur
    tasks add kar sakein.

      Completed)

================================================================================
  11. TASKS KAISE ADD, EDIT, YA DELETE KAREIN
================================================================================

Tasks (jise "Action Items" bhi kehte hain) woh kaam hain jo meeting
ke baad karne hote hain. Inhe kaise manage karein:

--- NAYA TASK ADD KARNA ---
Step 1: Meeting details page kholein.
Step 2: "Action Items / Tasks" section tak scroll karein.
Step 3: [+ Add Task] pe click karein.
Step 4: Bharein:
          - Title: Kya karna hai
          - Description: Task ke baare mein aur detail
          - Responsible Person: Kaun karega
          - Responsible Email: Unka email address
          - Deadline: Kab tak karna hai
          - Status: Abhi ki haalat (naye task ke liye usually "Pending")
Step 5: Task save karne ke liye checkmark/save button pe click karein.

--- PEHLE SE MAUJOOD TASK EDIT KARNA ---
Step 1: List mein task dhundhein.
Step 2: Task pe pencil (edit) icon pe click karein.
Step 3: Zaroorat ke hisaab se koi bhi field badlein.
Step 4: Changes save karne ke liye checkmark/save button pe click karein.

--- TASK KA STATUS BADALNA ---
Step 1: List mein task dhundhein.
Step 2: Status dropdown pe click karein (jisme "Pending", "In Progress"
        wagairah likha hota hai)
Step 3: Naya status chunein.
Badlaav automatically save ho jaata hai.

  16. RECORDING / UPLOAD KE BAAD KYA HOTA HAI (AI PROCESS)
================================================================================

Jab aap audio/video recording upload karte hain ya browser se seedha
record karte hain, toh system ek automatic 6-stage process shuru
karta hai:

Stage 1 - AWAAZ SE TEXT (Transcription):
  Audio file ek cloud service ko bheji jaati hai jo ise sunkar har
  bole gaye shabd ko likhit mein badal deti hai. Ye Hindi, English,
  aur mixed Hindi-English (Hinglish) conversations ke saath kaam
  karta hai. Har speaker ko alag pehchana jaata hai (Speaker A,
  Speaker B, wagairah).

Stage 2 - STRATEGIC SAMAJHDAAR SUMMARY (AI Analysis):
  Likhe hue text ko ek AI assistant analyze karta hai jo "Strategic Intelligence" use karta hai:
    a) Agenda Mapping: Meeting mein jo baat hui use sidha "Official Agenda" se link kiya jaata hai.
    b) Noise Filtering: AI ab "Conversational Noise" (jaise 400g ya 500g ki mamuli galti) ko ignore karta hai aur sirf "Material Strategic Figures" (jaise Final ₹2 Crore Investment) par focus karta hai.
    c) Decision & Task Extraction: Dashboard ke liye point-wise summary banata hai jisme hamesha **'DECISIONS MADE'** aur **'RECOMMENDED TASKS'** ka alag section hota hai.
    d) Professional Hierarchy: Summary mein Headers Hamesha BOLD UPPERCASE honge taaki padhne mein asaani ho.

Stage 3 - DOCUMENT BANANA (PDF Generation):
  Teen PDF documents bante hain:
    a) Full Transcript - Har bola gaya shabd, line by line
    b) AI Audit Log - AI ne discussion ko kaise toda aur analyze kiya
    c) Executive Briefing - Ek saaf-suthri, padhne layak summary

Stage 4 - CLOUD STORAGE (Google Drive Upload):
  Teenon PDFs plus original audio recording Google Drive mein ek
  dedicated folder mein upload hoti hain. Har meeting ka apna
  alag folder banta hai.

Stage 5 - DATABASE UPDATE:
  System mein meeting ka record upload ki gayi saari files ke
  links aur generated summary ke saath update hota hai.

Stage 6 - STATUS UPDATE:
  Jab ye sab ho raha hota hai, meeting ka status "Processing" rehta hai.
  Jab aap final MOM save karte hain (attendance aur tasks ke saath),
  toh har attendee ko ek professional email jaata hai jisme:
    - Email ke andar discussion summary
    - MOM PDF email ke saath attached
  Jinko task assign hua hai, unko alag email jaata hai jisme:
    - Kaunsa task unko assign hua
    - Kis meeting se
    - Deadline kab hai

Poori process usually 2-5 minute leti hai, recording ki length ke
hisaab se.

  24. AKSAR POOCHHE JAANE WALE SAWAAL (FAQ)
================================================================================

Q: Kya Botivate use karne ke liye account banana padega?
A: Abhi ke version mein koi login ki zaroorat nahi hai. System seedha
   web address se accessible hai.

Q: Audio ke kaunse formats upload kar sakte hain?
A: System .webm, .mp3, .m4a, .wav, aur .mp4 files accept karta hai.

Q: AI processing mein kitna time lagta hai?
A: Aamtaur pe 30-60 minute ki meeting recording ke liye 2-5 minute.
   Lambi recordings mein zyada time lag sakta hai.

Q: Kya AI Hindi meetings samajh sakta hai?
A: Haan! System Hindi, English, aur mixed Hindi-English (Hinglish)
   conversations samajhta hai. Summary hamesha professional English
   mein banti hai, chahe meeting kisi bhi bhaasha mein hui ho.

Q: Agar galti se meeting delete kar di toh?
A: Maaf kijiye, deletion permanent hai aur wapas nahi ho sakta.
   Hamesha deletion confirmation box pe OK karne se pehle confirm
   kar lein.

Q: Kya logon ko ek se zyada emails milte hain?
A: Jab aap MOM save karte hain, toh har attendee ko EK email milta
   hai jisme summary aur PDF hai. Iske alaawa, agar kisi ko task
   mila hai, toh unhe EK alag task notification email milta hai.
   Toh zyada se zyada, ek aadmi ko 2 emails aate hain.

Q: Meeting ke documents kahan store hote hain?
A: Saare documents (recordings, transcripts, summaries) aapki
   company ke Google Drive mein organized folders mein store hote hain.

Q: Kya AI ke banaye hue summary ko edit kar sakte hain?
A: Haan! Discussion Summary poori tarah se editable hai. Edit button
   pe click karein, changes karein, aur Save pe click karein.

Q: Agar meeting schedule ki par attendees add karna bhool gaye?
A: Aap baad mein Record MOM page ke zariye attendees add kar sakte
   hain jab actually meeting ke minutes log kar rahe hon.

Q: Kaise pata chalega ki AI abhi tak process kar raha hai?
A: Meeting ka status dekhein. Agar "Processing" dikha raha hai
   (ek dhadakti hui animation ke saath), toh AI abhi kaam kar raha
   hai. Hone ke baad aap MOM save kar sakte hain.

  25. IS MANUAL MEIN USE HONE WALE SHABDON KI DICTIONARY
================================================================================

MOM (Minutes of Meeting):
  Meeting mein jo discuss hua aur decide hua, uska likha hua record.

Board Resolution (BR):
  Kisi company ke board of directors dwara liya gaya formal faisla.
  Meeting jaisi hi hoti hai par governance aur compliance ke niyam
  laagu hote hain.

Attendee:
  Woh aadmi jo meeting mein bulaya gaya hai ya meeting mein aata hai.

Action Item / Task:
  Woh kaam jo meeting ke baad karna hota hai. Ek khaas aadmi
  zimmedaar hota hai aur ek deadline hoti hai.

Agenda:
  Meeting mein discuss kiye jaane wale topics ki pehle se bani hui list.

Discussion Summary:
  Meeting mein kya baat hui uska likha hua saransh.

Transcript:
  Har bole gaye shabd ki likhi hui nakal. Jaise asli
  baatcheet ko kaagaz pe padh rahe ho.

Dashboard:
  Home page jo saari meetings, tasks, aur statistics ka overview dikhata hai.

Status (Meeting):
  - Scheduled: Meeting future ke liye plan ki gayi hai.
  - Processing: AI abhi meeting ki recording analyze kar raha hai.
  - Completed: Meeting ho gayi aur MOM record ho chuka hai.
  - Rescheduled: Meeting ki date/time badal di gayi hai.
  - Cancelled: Meeting ab nahi hogi.

Status (Task):
  - Pending: Task abhi shuru nahi hua hai.
  - In Progress: Koi us task pe kaam kar raha hai abhi.
  - Completed: Task pura ho chuka hai.
  - Overdue: Deadline nikal gayi par task complete nahi hua.

PDF:
  Ek document format jo formatting preserve karta hai aur asaani
  se print ya share kiya ja sakta hai. System har meeting ke liye
  professional PDF documents banata hai.

Google Drive:
  Google ki ek online storage service jahan meeting ke saare
  documents safely store hote hain aur kahin se bhi access kiye
  ja sakte hain.
